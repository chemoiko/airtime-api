About Edoctorug

Welcome to Edoctorug, where we believe in bringing healthcare closer to you. Our mission is to revolutionize healthcare access by providing telemedicine services and essential medical products at your fingertips.

At Edoctorug, we understand the challenges of accessing quality healthcare, especially in remote areas or during emergencies. That's why we've built a platform that connects patients with medical specialists and facilitates seamless delivery of healthcare services and products, all from the comfort of your home.

Our comprehensive suite of services includes:

  • Online doctor consultations: Speak to experienced medical professionals without leaving your home.
  • Online medicine orders: Easily order medicines and have them delivered to your doorstep.
  • Online ambulance bookings: Arrange for emergency medical transportation with just a few clicks.
  • Online lab test orders: Schedule and receive lab test results conveniently.
  • Chronic diseases monitoring: Monitor and manage chronic conditions with regular support from our healthcare experts.
  • Online diseases testing: Get tested for various illnesses without visiting a clinic.
  • Medical loan applications: Access financial assistance tailored for medical expenses.
  • Medical care savings: Save and budget for future healthcare needs with our savings plan.
  • Airtime and mobile money APIs: Seamlessly integrate airtime top-up and mobile money services into your applications.

Whether you're seeking medical advice, ordering medicines, or monitoring your health, Edoctorug is here to ensure that you receive prompt, reliable, and compassionate care.

Join us in our mission to make healthcare accessible to all, regardless of location or circumstance. Together, we can build a healthier, happier world.

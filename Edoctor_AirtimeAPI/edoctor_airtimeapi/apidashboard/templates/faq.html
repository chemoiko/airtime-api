Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Edoctorug?

Edoctorug is your go-to telemedicine service that connects you with medical specialists and provides access to health services and products from the comfort of your home.

How can I consult with a doctor online?

It's easy! Just use our website or mobile app to schedule an appointment. A medical specialist will be ready to help you within minutes.

How do I order medicines online?

Ordering medicines is simple. Visit our website or app, browse through the available medicines, and place your order. We'll deliver them right to your doorstep.

Can I book an ambulance online?

Yes, you can. Book an ambulance through our platform for quick and reliable transportation to healthcare facilities in emergencies.

What types of lab tests can I order online?

We offer a wide range of lab tests, including blood tests, urine tests, and more. Order online and receive your results electronically.

How does the chronic diseases monitoring service work?

Our service helps you track your health metrics and receive regular check-ins from medical professionals. This ensures timely interventions and better management of chronic conditions.

What is online diseases testing?

Our online diseases testing allows you to book various tests through our platform and receive quick, confidential results without visiting a healthcare facility.

Can I apply for a medical loan through Edoctorug?

Absolutely! You can apply for a medical loan via our platform. We connect you with financial institutions offering loans tailored for medical expenses.

What is the medical care savings plan?

Our medical care savings plan lets you save money for future healthcare needs. Regularly set aside funds and use them when needed for medical expenses.

What is the airtime API?

Our airtime API allows businesses to integrate airtime top-up services into their applications, providing a seamless way to recharge mobile phones.

What is the mobile money API?

Our mobile money API facilitates easy and secure mobile money transactions within your applications, making it convenient for users to handle their finances.

What additional services does Edoctorug offer?

In addition to our core healthcare services, we offer an airtime API and a mobile money API, making transactions and connectivity easier for businesses and individuals.
